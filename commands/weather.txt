---------------
| WEATHER COMMANDS |
---------------

,[forecast|fe] [city_name]
└─ Gets some forecast.
,[weather|we] [city_name]
└─ Gets the weather.
,[temperature|temp] [city_name]
└─ Get the temperature only!
